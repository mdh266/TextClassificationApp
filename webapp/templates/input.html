About:

This app will classify computer science papers from their abstract and predict its topic using machine learning model hosted on Google Cloud Run.

How To Use:

Go to arXiv.org website using one of links on the following topics:

  • Artifical Intelligence
  • Computer Vision
  • Machine Learning
  • Robitics
Click on the link for paper of your choice then copy and paste the abstract of paper into the form below.
